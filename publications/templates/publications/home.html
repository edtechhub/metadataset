Meta-analysis of open data

Metadataset is a collection of open data that are used for meta-analysis. The data are extracted from scientific publications, transformed into standardized effect sizes, tagged with standardized metadata, and aggregated into open-source meta-analyses.

Systematic review of agricultural practices

Our mission is to create a comprehensive collection of standardized data about agricultural practices. Thanks to funding from the Leventis Foundation, we are starting by systematically reviewing scientific publications about cassava.
