Meta-analysis of open data

Metadataset is a collection of open data from scientific experiments. The data are extracted from scientific publications, transformed into standardized effect sizes, tagged with standardized metadata, and synthesized using meta-analysis.

Systematic reviews of agricultural practices

Our mission is to create a standardized collection of systematic reviews of agricultural practices. Thanks to funding from the Leventis Foundation, we are starting by systematically mapping scientific publications about cassava.
