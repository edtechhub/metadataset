Meta-analysis of open data

Metadataset is a collection of open data that are used for meta-analysis. The data are extracted from scientific publications, transformed into standardized effect sizes, and tagged with standardized metadata. Subsets of the data are aggregated into open-source meta-analyses that are moderated by our community of Data Wranglers.

Systematic reviews of agricultural practices

Our mission is to create a comprehensive collection of standardized data about sustainable agricultural practices. Thanks to funding from the Leventis Foundation, we are starting this mission by systematically reviewing scientific publications about cassava.
